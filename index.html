Welcome to the Phantom Grid Sim Racing Project

About Us

Welcome to the Phantom Grid Sim Racing Project! This started as a passion project for me to fuse the excitement of sim racing with a software development project leveraging the creativity of AI.

The goal of this project is to compete in full season championships with AI components and track the excitement of the season as it progresses. Each of the opponents as a full backstory, which can be read on the website in the Driver Profiles area fpr their respective season. I plan to create new rosters for each season that I create, focusing on different cars and classes. As the season progresses, rivalries will emerge as everyone jockies for position at the top of the table. I will also be one of the drivers and will include my own experiences in the race summaries.

Using Generative AI and automation, each week's race results will be parsed from the iRacing logs and updated on the website. In addition to individual driver standings, a team championship will also be calculated. Drivers are placed in 2-man teams based on their car manufacturer. The driver profiles and team names were created entirely by Generative AI, along with the driver profile pictures.

Check out the Tech Stuff pages to learn more about the engineering and software development process for managing all of the content. Since I will be racing alongside the AI opponents, I have also created a page on my sim racing rig, accessories, and setup.

Project Goals

  • Use Generative AI to create and manage a complete website
  • Use Generative AI to create race summaries and process race results
  • Create a workflow and dev pipeline to easily manage all of the necessary updates
  • Add text to speech to create driver interviews (future)
  • Add temperment ratings to the drivers to determine the tone of their interviews (future)
