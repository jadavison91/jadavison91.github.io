Welcome to the Phantom Grid Sim Racing Project

About Us

Welcome to the Phantom Grid Sim Racing Project! This started as a passion project for me to fuse the excitement of sim racing with a software development project leveraging the creativity of AI.

The goal of this project is to compete in full season championships with AI components and track the excitement of the season as it progresses. Each of the opponents as a full backstory, which can be read on the website in the Driver Profiles area. As the season progresses, rivalries will emerge as everyone jockies for position at the top of the table. I will also be one of the drivers and will include my own experiences in the race summaries.

Using Generative AI and automation, each week's race results will be parsed from the iRacing logs and updated on the website. In addition to individual driver standings, each team will consist of two drivers in the same car. The driver profiles and team names were created entirely by Generative AI, along with the driver profile pictures.

Project Goals

  • Use Generative AI to create and manage a complete website
  • Use Generative AI to create race summaries and process race results
  • Create a workflow and dev pipeline to easily manage all of the necessary updates
  • Add text to speech to create driver interviews (future)
  • Add temperment ratings to the drivers to determine the tone of their interviews (future)
